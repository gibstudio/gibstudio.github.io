Privacy Policy

We do not collect or store any information on your use of our apps. We do not track your usage, location, or any other information which would tell us who uses this app. Once our applications are installed on your phone, they do not send any personal information to our servers in any way.

For our free applications, we use Admob for mobile advertising. We do not sell any information regarding our users. For our paid applications, we do not use advertising.

Due to the ways the app markets work, however, we receive information regarding your store details when you purchase one of our apps. All mobile apps receive this information (not just the apps belonging to us). However, we do not collect or store this information.

We strongly believe that you are entitled to your own privacy. We will always respect your privacy.
